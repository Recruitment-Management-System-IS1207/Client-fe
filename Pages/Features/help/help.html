What can we help you find?

How do I post a job opening? +
To post a job opening, please contact our system administrators at admin@pathfinder.com with your job details. Our admin team will review and post the job listing on your behalf to ensure quality and consistency.
How long does it take to get a job posting approved? +
Our admin team typically reviews and posts job listings within 24-48 hours of receiving the request. For urgent postings, please mention this in your email subject line.
What information should I include in my job posting request? +
Please include: job title, company details, location, salary range, required qualifications, job description, responsibilities, benefits, and application deadline. The more detailed information you provide, the faster we can process your request.
How can I modify or remove a job posting? +
To modify or remove an existing job posting, email our admin team at admin@pathfinder.com with your request. Include the job ID or title and the specific changes needed. Updates are usually processed within 24 hours.
Is there a limit to how many jobs I can post? +
There's no strict limit, but all postings are subject to admin review. Contact our admin team to discuss your recruitment needs, and they'll help create a posting schedule that works for your organization.
How do I track applications for my job postings? +
Our admin team will forward all applications to your designated email address. For customized application tracking or reporting needs, please discuss with our admin team when submitting your job posting.
